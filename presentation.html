Key Features of the Digital Library System

I. Centralized Digital Access
All library book details are stored in one digital platform, making access faster and more organized for students.
II. Department-Wise Segregation
Books are arranged based on individual departments, helping students quickly find relevant academic resources.
III. Time-Saving for Students
Students can check book availability without physically searching shelves, reducing time spent inside the library.
IV. User-Friendly Interface
The website is designed with a simple layout, clear navigation, and readable fonts for easy usage.
V. Improved Book Management
Library staff can manage book records efficiently with reduced manual errors.
